AKCSSC | #BeTheRift

AKCSSC | #BeTheRift

It's always the small pieces that make the big picture. Our perspectives, and their differences, make for an interesting array of solutions. It might not be realized at first with a number of seemingly unrelated pieces, but they are all interconnected. Sometimes, the big picture means looking small, finding all the pieces, and understanding that there is more to come. We ourselves can be the individual piece that completes someone else's picture.
